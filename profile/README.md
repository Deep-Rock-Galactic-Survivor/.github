## About Deep Rock Galactic: Survivor

Deep Rock Galactic: Survivor is a single-player auto-shooter survival action game set in the sci-fi mining universe of Deep Rock Galactic. Players descend into hostile underground zones, fight off massive alien swarms, gather valuable resources, and attempt to survive increasingly intense combat encounters while progressing through fast-paced roguelite runs.

The game focuses on automatic weapon fire, movement-based survival, mining, and upgrade decisions made during each session. Players collect minerals, improve gear, choose enhancements, and adapt their build as enemy pressure rises. Different classes, weapon types, and progression options create varied runs and encourage experimentation across repeated attempts.

With short replayable sessions, escalating enemy waves, resource collection, class-based combat, and roguelite upgrade systems, Deep Rock Galactic: Survivor delivers a high-action survival experience centered on momentum, build planning, and underground sci-fi chaos.
